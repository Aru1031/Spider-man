Facts about Spider-Man

  1. Spider-Man was created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko.
    Spider-Man was created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko. You might recognise Stan Lee as he has made a lot cameos in Marvel movies!
  2. Spider-Man first appeared in 1962.
    Everyone’s favourite web-slinger first appeared in August 1962 in a comic called Amazing Fantasy #15. That means Spider-Man is nearly 60-years-old!
  3. It’s Spider-Man, not Spider Man.
    It’s Spider-Man, not Spider Man. Stan Lee specifically placed a hyphen between Spider and Man to avoid confusion with another super hero, Superman!
  4. Spider-Man is loved by a former President.
    Spider-Man is loved by a former President. Spidey is former U.S President Barack Obama’s favourite superhero!
  5. There have been nine Spider-Man movies made.
    There have been nine Spider-Man movies made. They are: Spider-Man (2002), Spider-Man 2 (2004), Spider-Man 3 (2007), The Amazing Spider-Man (2012), The Amazing Spider-Man 2 (2014), Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017), Spider-Man: Into the Spider-verse (2018), Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019), and Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021).
  6. Three more Spider-Man films have been announced.
    Three more Spider-Man films have been announced. There’s not much information out there atm, other than the fact we can expect three more Spidey movies!
